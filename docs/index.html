Class-D Perks

Medic

Instant recovery for you and your teammates.

Utility:

  • MediBomb: Affects the user and Class-Ds in its radius with regeneration.
  • x2 Medic Bag: Regenerates 8 hearts upon drinking the potion.

MediBomb Stats:

  • Duration: 10 seconds
  • Cooldown: 40 seconds
  • Radius: 5 blocks

Painkiller

Extra health and temporary invulnerability.

Utility:

  • Ibuprofen Spray: Renders Class-Ds in its radius invulnerable, excluding the user. Affects the user with glowing when deployed.
  • High Dosage Painkiller: Provides 8 hearts of absorption for 45 seconds upon drinking the potion.

Ibuprofen Spray Stats:

  • Glowing Duration: 10 seconds
  • Invulnerability Duration: 15 seconds
  • Radius: 5 blocks
  • Cooldown: 40 seconds
  • Base health: 24 HP

Exposer

Reveal enemy locations and gain speed boost.

Utility:

The Exposer: Speeds you up, blinds nearby enemies, and exposes them.

Stats:

  • Range: 5 blocks
  • Blindness Duration: 3 seconds
  • Speed Duration: 7 seconds
  • Cooldown: 60 seconds

Speed-o

Increased movement speed and energy drinks.

Utility:

x2 Energy Drink: Increases movement speed by 60% for 10 seconds. Affects the user with glowing for 10 seconds.

Stats:

  • Base movement speed: +5%
  • Base health: 16 HP

Utility Master

Various tools to support team and stall enemies.

Utility:

  • Adrenaline Bomb: Speeds up players in its radius by 40% for 45 seconds.
  • x2 Molotov: Sets the thrown area on fire, dealing significant damage to enemies caught in it or passing through.
  • x2 Smoke Grenade: Blinds players near impact and creates a smoke cloud that breaks line of sight.

MTF Perks

Shotgunner

FP6 shotgun for close-range termination.

Equipped with a FP6 shotgun. Multishot property allows hitting multiple targets up close. Rockets deal area of effect damage.

Utility:

Gas grenade: Makes areas inconvenient for Class-D and SCPs to pass through.

Stats:

  • Ammo Count: 32 bullets
  • Reload Speed: 0.75 second

Scout

MP5 submachine gun for suppression.

Equipped with a MP5 submachine gun. Extremely fast fire rate and large ammunition quantity for overwhelming targets.

Stats:

  • Ammo Count: 90 bullets
  • Reload Speed: instant

Note: Weaker firepower compared to other guns. Targets regenerate most health if they flee.

Sniper

SSG 08 bolt action rifle for long-range combat.

Equipped with SSG 08 bolt action sniper rifle. Slow fire rate but high damage.

Utility:

Spyglass: Allows zooming for better long-range vision.

Stats:

  • Ammo Count: 20 bullets
  • Reload Speed: 1.25 seconds
  • Stun Duration: 1.25 seconds

Note: Targets are stunned when hit and take longer to recover health compared to other guns.

Infiltrator

M1911 handgun and enemy-revealing ability.

Equipped with the standard M1911 handgun. Supports the team by exposing locations of Class-Ds and SCPs.

Utility:

The Observer: Reveals SCPs and Class-Ds for 40 seconds or until they crouch for 3 seconds. 60 seconds cooldown.

Stats:

  • Ammo Count: 27 bullets
  • Reload Speed: 0.75 seconds

Quake Pro

M1911 handgun with enhanced speed.

Equipped with the standard M1911 handgun. Features enhanced speed for quick target engagement.

Utility:

Adrenaline Bomb: Speeds up players in its radius by 40% for 45 seconds.

Stats:

  • Ammo Count: 18 bullets
  • Reload Speed: 0.75 seconds
  • Movement Speed: +10%
