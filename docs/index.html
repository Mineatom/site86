Class-D Perks

Medic

Medics can help both themselves and their teammates recover instantly from rough encounters, with the use of their MediBomb.


Utility:

  • MediBomb; effects the user and Class-Ds in its radius with regeneration.
  • x2 Medic Bag; regenerates 8 hearts upon drinking the potion.

MediBomb stats
Duration - 10 seconds
Cooldown - 40 seconds
Radius - 5 blocks

Painkiller

Painkiller is the go-to perk if you're looking to save your teammates from dire situations with its extra health and Ibuprofen Spray.


Utility:

  • Ibuprofen Spray; Renders Class-Ds in its radius, excluding the user, invulnerable. And it will affect the user with glowing when deployed.
  • High Dosage Painkiller; provides 8 hearts of absorbtion for 45 seconds upon drinking the potion.

Ibuprofen Spray stats
Glowing Duration - 10 seconds
Invulnerability Duration - 15 seconds
Radius - 5 blocks
Cooldown - 40 seconds

Exposer

Looking for ways to avoid the MTF altogether? The Exposer will allow you to do just that and more, by revealing their location.


Utility: The Exposer; speeds you up, blinds nearby enemies and exposes them.


Range - 5 blocks
Blindness Duration - 3 seconds
Speed Duration - 7 seconds
Cooldown - 60 seconds
Base health - 24 HP

Speed-o

You'll be.. speedy, with Speed-o. If you're good at avoiding damage, you'll escape in no time with this perk.


Utility: x2 Energy Drink; increases your movement speed by 40% for 12 seconds. However, it will affect its user with glowing for 5 seconds along with it.


Base movement speed - +10%
Base health - 16 HP

Utility Master

The Utility Master is all about supporting their team and stalling the enemy, equipped with a variety of tools to their advantage.


Utility:

  • Adrenaline Bomb; speeds up whoever in its radius by 40% for 45 seconds.
  • x2 Molotov; will set wherever it's thrown on fire, dealing significant damage to enemies caught in it, or whoever dares to go through it.
  • x2 Smoke Grenade; when thrown, it will blind players next to it on impact and create a cloud of smoke that breaks the line of sight.

MTF Perks

Shotgunner

Shotgunner is equipped with a FP6 shotgun. With its multishot property, it is able to easily hit multiple targets up close and terminate them quickly if shot consecutively. If you take too long between shots on your target however, you will end up dealing much lower damage.


Utility: Gas grenade; used to make it more inconvenient for Class-D and SCPs to pass through areas you don't want them going through.


Ammo Count - 24 bullets
Reload Speed - 1 second

Scout

Scout is equipped with a MP5 submachine gun. With its extremely fast fire rate and large quantity of ammunition, you can easily overwhelm and suppress your targets. The MP5 has weaker firepower compared to other guns, and targets will regenerate most of their health if they manage to flee.


Ammo Count - 90 bullets
Reload Speed - instant

Sniper

Sniper is equipped with SSG 08 bolt action sniper rifle. It may have a slow fire rate, but it hits like a truck. Targets will be stunned when hit, and will take way longer to recover their health compared to when hit with other guns.


Utility: Spyglass, allows you to zoom and see better at range. Or you could just use optifine I guess...


Ammo Count - 20 bullets
Reload Speed - 1.25 seconds
Stun Duration - 1.25 seconds

Infiltrator

Equipped with the standard M1911 handgun, The Infiltrator supports their team by exposing locations of Class-Ds and SCPs.


Utility: The Observer; reveals SCPs and Class-Ds for 40 seconds or until they crouch for 3 seconds. 60 seconds cooldown.


Ammo Count - 27 bullets
Reload Speed - 0.75 seconds

Quake Pro

Quake Pro is equipped with the standard M1911 handgun. What's special about the Quake Pro however, is their speed. Once you catch up to your target, you can be sure that they will not be getting away any time soon.


Utility: Adrenaline Bomb; speeds up whoever in its radius by 40% for 45 seconds.


Ammo Count - 18 bullets
Reload Speed - 0.75 seconds
Movement Speed - +10%
